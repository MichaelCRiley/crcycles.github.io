About Me

My name is Michael Riley and welcome to C & R Cycles! C & R Cycles was born from busted knuckles and built on passion. My Fiancé and I have a true love for riding and the adventure that is life on two wheels. I have been blessed with the opportunity to wrench on bikes from every style and major manufacturer. I aim to deliver good quality work that allows other riders to be safe on the road, track, or wherever your next adventure may take you!
